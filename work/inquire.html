TLDR

I, with the help of my team, designed and built a central user data service for personalization of more than 100 local newspapers. The service integrated data usage monitoring to give data owners high-resolution insight into what data was consumed by who, user consent information to give consumers confidence that they were using data correctly, and data governance to ensure the data platform owners that the service provided up-to-date and valid information. Furthermore, to remove the platform team as a bottleneck when adding user attributes, the service was designed to be as simple to administrate as possible, and includes a well-thought-out management panel.
